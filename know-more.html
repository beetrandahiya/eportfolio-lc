About the Module!

The module is called LHTL- Learning How To Learn.
It covers the study of the fundamental principles of learning,
the learning process and the learning tools.
The module is designed to help students to understand
learning in the right way in order to be more productive and efficient
not just during our Academic Life but also in our Career.
Various interactive methods will be employed throughout the course
to make learning exciting and intresting
