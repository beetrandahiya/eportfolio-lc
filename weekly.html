KEY ATTRIBUTES EMPLOYERS WANT TO SEE ON STUDENTS' RESUMES

Recent hiring trends suggest that employers are more focused on the abilities of employees to communicate, work in teams, b accountable for ones own work, amongst other key soft skills. These skills are relevant through all jobs profile. Soft skills are key to building relations, gaining visibility and creating opportunities. Soft skills can be taught to an individual but is not: and easy concept the fathom unlike the hard skills. Regardless of the industry one belongs to or ones field of expertise one requires soft skills to sustain and succeed in job market.
  • Communication skills: These are the most crucial skills that are requires to sustain oneself in todays market . Employers look for candidates who are not only well educated and are intellectually efficient , but for candidates who have a good grasp over the language, can channel their thoughts well and know how to communicate their ideas to the larger audience.
  • Emotional Intelligence: The ability to recognize and manage ones emotions and understand others emotions is imperative when working with a diverse group of people. It allows for employees to judge situations that need to be handled with care and act accordingly.
  • Teamwork: It is very rare for a person to work alone , meaning , one must communicate well, actively participate in conversation and respect perspectives. From playing with blocks at playschool, to making school projects with classmates we have actually been preparing ourselves for a lifetime of workplace collaborations.
  • Flexibilty: Flexibility is a key skill that is required to be able to adapt to a changing environment. It is also important to be able to adapt to a changing environment. world is transforming at a rapid pace . The dynamics are changing at a tremendous pace. Being prepared is the key to a tackling challenges , handling transitions or adjusting to novel circumstances and business requirements.
  • Leadership : Leadership is not just about being a team lead, rather it is a trait of taking ownership. The one who is authoritative in managing the team , lead the team by example and get the work done by assigning individuals of the team into different job roles are the ones who are most likely to be selected.
